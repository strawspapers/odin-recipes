Biscuits and Gravy

Southern style biscuits with sausage gravy

Hot jumbo buttermilk biscuits with creamy sausage gravy are ready in just 15 minutes for a hearty, family-favorite breakfast

Ingredients

  • (1)- 16oz can buttermilk biscuits
  • 1lb pork breakfast sausage
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 2 1/2 cups milk
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare biscuits according to package
  2. Cook sausage over medium heat until crumbly and fat well rendered
  3. Remove sausage from pan leaving behind rendered fat
  4. add flour and cook until raw flavor is gone, approximately 4 minutes
  5. slowly whisk in milk until roux turns to a loose gravy
  6. return sausage to gravy, mix to incorporate
  7. serve gravy over hot biscuits
